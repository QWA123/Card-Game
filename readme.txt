There are two decks of 26 cards each.
A move consists of both players(Computer and Human) removing a card from the top of their respective decks and exposing their values.
The player who has a greater numeric value on their card wins on this move and adds the cards from both his and the opponent's deck to his own deck.
If the numeric values are same then this move is draw and the decks are restored to their previous state.
The player who first gains all 52 cards wins the game.
